Filmmaking textbook

Film making textbook by walrus is a guide book that teaches us how to make films, both for beginners and experienced ones. In this book we are taught the procedures for taking correct lighting, taking the right camera shots, and many others. This book is very useful for beginners, because what is taught is very detailed.
