Ruang rasa

"Ruang rasa" is the title of a coming soon digital poster that aims to invite millennials to attend a short da'wah via zoom. In this zoom we will be invited to get to know the religion of Islam more deeply and learn to be grateful for the blessings that Allah has given us all. And the zoom will be led by Mr. Sonny Abi Kim who is the co-founder of the PPA institute.
