Mamah Aisyah

Keripik lezat mamah aisyah is a small brand for a home business that sells chips and various other dry foods. The food that is sold also varies, some are spicy or sweet.

Because this business focuses more on chips, the logo design is jagged like the sun with a combination of orange to indicate sharp chips to make it look more attractive and not flat. And there is an illustration of a woman in the logo to make it easier to remember and people will immediately understand the name of the shop.
